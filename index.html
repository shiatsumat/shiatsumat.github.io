Yusuke MATSUSHITA (松下 祐介)


(Last updated: Mar 11, 2025)

I am a software scientist. I specialize in formal verification of stateful programs, especially those written in Rust (e.g., my work RustHorn and RustHornBelt). I am also curious about many other topics, including algorithms and natural language processing (e.g., SoftMatcha).

From April 2025, I will be a Program-Specific Assistant Professor at the Hakubi Center for Advanced Research, Kyoto University. Until March 2025, I work as a JSPS Fellow (PD) at the Computer Software Group, Grad School of Informatics, Kyoto University.
